ABOUT PARTLY

This project is a database application for a place for people to buy car parts from suppliers. The primary objective is to ensure a seamless user experience throughout the process of purchasing car parts. This includes making a well-structured and organized database, allowing for fast searching and sorting. With this, transactions are aimed to be effortless and secure while also allowing for well-regulated order tracking and management.

Additionally, with the implementation of accessible interfaces, the prime motive of this project is to simplify the process of purchasing parts for everyone. This will ensure a satisfying shopping experience. Our objective to create a user-friendly and uncomplicated system to effortlessly search and purchase any car part(s) will prioritize leaving customers feeling content and pleased.
